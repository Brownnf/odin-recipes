Tacos

Description

Family recipe for tacos.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger meat
  • Taco seasoning
  • Salsa
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Put hamburger meat into a skillet on medium heat
  2. Break meat up into small bite size pieces
  3. Cook until brown
  4. Pour off the grease and replace with water
  5. Stir in taco seasoning and salsa
  6. Serve and enjoy
