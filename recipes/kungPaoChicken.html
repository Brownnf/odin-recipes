Kung Pao Chicken

Savory Kung Pao Chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • White rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Hot sauce
  • Water
  • Sesame seed oil
  • Green pepper
  • Carrot
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Cut up the vegetables into small chunks
  2. Cut chicken into small bite size pieces
  3. Dump chicken into a skillet on medium heat
  4. Combine your liquid ingredients into a bowl
  5. Cook until chicken is mostly done
  6. Pour off the grease and add the vegetables
  7. Stir in the sause
  8. Serve and enjoy
