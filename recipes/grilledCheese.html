Grilled Cheese

Delicious Grilled Cheese.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread and cover 1 side with butter
  2. Place 1 piece of bread facing with the butter down in a skilled on medium heat
  3. Put the American cheese on the bread and then cover it with the other slice of buttered bread
  4. Wait until you can see the butter on top melting, then flip the grilled cheese
  5. Wait 2 more minutes then remove from skillet and enjoy
